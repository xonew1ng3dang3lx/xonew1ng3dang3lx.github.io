Japan Trip

Cool sights to visit during your visit to japan

Tokyo One Piece Tower

If you love One Piece or anime in general, you can't miss out on this indoor theme park. There are lots of interactive displays and One Piece Live attraction shows where characters tell adventurous stories on stage.

Nijo-jo Castle

Nijo Castle is arguable one of the castle palace architecture during Japan's feudal era. It is divided into three areas: the Honmaru, Ninomaru, and the garden. The entire castle is surrounded by stone walls and moats. Visitors can enter Nijo Castle and see its beautiful interior. (Translators are present for visitors as well which makes the experience much more inspiring!)
